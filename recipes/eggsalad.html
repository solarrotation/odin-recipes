Egg Salad

Return to Homepage

Description

This recipe is a classic! Create an egg salad with your older eggs and add to a sandwhich for a delicious lunch!

Ingredients

  • 8 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper

Instructions

  1. Rinse peeled hard boiled eggs, and pat dry.
  2. In a bowl, chop or mash peeled eggs.
  3. Add mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper. Mix well!
  4. You're finished! Dip in french bread, make a sandwhich, add to crackers, or make a salad! Enjoy!
