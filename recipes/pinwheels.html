Turkey Bacon Ranch Pinwheels

Return to Homepage

Description

Quick, easy, delicious! These pinwheels are stuffed with delicious turkey, mouthwatering bacon, and homemade ranch cheese spread!

Ingredients

  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of dry Ranch seasoning
  • 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 9 ounces packaged deli turkey
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the Ranch seasoning with the cream cheese, cheddar cheese, and chopped bacon.
  2. Spread the cream cheese mixture on the tortilla.
  3. Layer the deli turkey on top of the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Tightly roll the tortilla and wrap in plastic wrap, then chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the rolled tortillas from the fridge, and slice into 1inch thick pinwheels.
  6. Enjoy!
